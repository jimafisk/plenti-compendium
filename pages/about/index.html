About Me

I was born in the land of Oz, and had always dreamed that a hurricane would take me somewhere afar. Eventually I actually ended up working abroad, where I began traveling throughout Asia. After discovering the world of low-cost airlines, I started seeing the world every weekend.

Once I stayed in the 15th country I had visited, I realized that I had enough experience and knowledge to share. Here you will find useful tips, my travel experiences, life hacks and the usual daily thoughts about everything that surrounds me.
